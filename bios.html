Nico Lee

Drummer

Nic was born in San Franscisco. He grew up with a love for surfing, music and science. By age 12 he had already learned 4 instruments and started highschool. He would go on to graduate college with a bachelor's by 19 in molecular Biology. Shortly after graduating the college band gigs he had played for fun turned into something more real when he was asked to audition for the newly formed band Salt Blossom. And the rest is history. When he's not touring he still lives in SF, with his two dogs and a cockatoo named Shirley.

Jack Foster

Singer/SongWriter

Jack was born in Kansas City and first got into music when he saw his first concert with his mom, Alkaline Trio. Two years later, he picked up his first guitar and didn't practice much to his father's lament. It wasn't until he started singing that he found his passion. Playing in a few local bands in high school and college, it wasn't until his senior year that he realised finance wasn't for him and formed the band Saltblossom.

Kenzie Dyson

Lead Guitar

Kenzie grew up in a very musical household. Her mother and father were both musicians in their downtime and her sister is a small time DJ when not working as a record producer, so She was exposed to a lot of the music industry from a very young age. Kenzie spends her downtime with her Girlfriend and two cats, Fluffybutt and Shadow. As the newest member of the band, Kenzie is still getting her bearings but she hopes to make the fans proud..
